About me

A geek at heart

My name is Chuks and I was born and raised in Nigeria. I came to the United States when I was very young with my family. And as a first-generation immigrant child I had three career options: a doctor, an engineer or a disgrace. But like most kids that grew up in the 90s I was into video games and computers. Alot. I still have fond memories of my sega genesis and nintendo 64. My love for electronics only grew from there. My parents weren't a fan of my 'obsession', they thold me that I could never make money playing video games -- boy were they wrong. I never grew out of it and now I've taken that 'obsession' and made it into my career. My parents could not be anymore proud.

Dancer, Chef and Programmer

I am a former biology major who decided to puruse his passion as a programmer. I have been programming since 2016. I am well versed in multiple languages such as; JavaScript, React, React-native, HTML/CSS, MongoDB, Node.js, MySQL, and more. I am also an incredible dancer/choregrapher who has performed around the world. And not to mention a mean cook. So when you ever need a programmer that can moonwalk and whip up a mean stir fry then I'm your guy.
